// Our Origin
WHY WE
BUILT
SPARKUP

SparkUp Creative was born from a frustration: the gap between what AI can do and what most businesses are actually doing with it. While Fortune 500s were deploying intelligent systems, small businesses and entrepreneurs were left behind — overwhelmed by hype, underwhelmed by practical guidance.

"We don't just use AI — we build with it, teach it, and deploy it to drive real results for real people."

Founded by Yaser Shamim with co-founder Areeba Hasan, SparkUp Creative is a Wyoming-registered LLC operating at the intersection of AI innovation and human-centred growth. We serve businesses globally from our base in Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh, India.

Our Women Empowerment Initiative reflects our deepest value: AI education as a force for equity. Every batch of 10 women we train is a testament that the AI revolution belongs to everyone.
